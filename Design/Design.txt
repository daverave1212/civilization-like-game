
    Gameplay
You start with a town and you generate resources every turn.
If you have a tile, you have it and it contributes to resource gain.


        Resources

Each base terrain tile produces 3 resources

    Primary
- Food
    - Buy tiles
- Gold
    - Everything also costs gold
- Materials
    - Construct improvement buildings on tiles you own
- Might
    - Clear neutral camps around

    Secondary
- Magic



        Capturable Tiles
Fish - Increases Food production by 10%
Crystals - Increases Gold production by 10%
Elemental Rift - Increases Magic production by 10%
